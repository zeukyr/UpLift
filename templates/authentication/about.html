WHO ARE WE

ABOUT US

It's often a lonely and confusing experience to be a first generation student — but it doesn't have to be. If you are looking to apply to a Canadian university, UpLift will connect you with people who have gone through the same process! If you're a current first-generation university student, you can join a supportive network of peers and exchange academic resources.


OUR VISION

We envision a community where first-generation students feel valued, supported, and connected, enabling them to overcome challenges and achieve their educational goals.


HOW IT WORKS

Getting involved with Uplift is easy! To be able to participate in discussions, all you have to do is make an account.


TESTIMONIALS
